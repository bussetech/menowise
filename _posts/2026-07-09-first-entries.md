Menowise's first [entries](/entries/) are live. This opening set covers core
menopause topics, each resolved from per-source signals and cited to named
clinical guidelines:

- **[Vasomotor symptoms](/entries/vasomotor-symptoms/)** (hot flushes, night sweats)
- **[Genitourinary syndrome of menopause](/entries/genitourinary-syndrome-menopause/)**
- **[Menopausal hormone therapy](/entries/menopausal-hormone-therapy/)**
- **[Local vaginal estrogen](/entries/vaginal-estrogen/)**
- **[Fezolinetant](/entries/fezolinetant/)**

Sources this batch: The Menopause Society's 2022 Hormone Therapy, 2023
Nonhormone Therapy, and 2020 Genitourinary Syndrome of Menopause position
statements, and the 2025 AUA/SUFU/AUGS guideline on GSM. Every entry carries an
honest evidence-strength label and its citations.

Deliberately few and careful, not broad — the corpus grows one cited entry at a
time. **Not medical advice:** menowise aggregates and organizes what the
literature reports; it does not diagnose, advise, or recommend treatment.
