**Menowise** aggregates and organizes **journal-evidenced** information on
menopause and midlife women's health, so it can be explored — by topic,
symptom, and intervention — with every claim tied to the literature.

The model is *per-source signals → cited entries*: claims are collected one per
source from peer-reviewed literature and clinical guidelines, then resolved into
canonical entries carrying an honest **evidence-strength** label. Where the
literature is thin, that is recorded as content, not hidden.

{% include callout.html label="Not medical advice" body="Menowise aggregates and organizes what the published literature reports, with citations. It does not diagnose, advise, or recommend treatment. Talk to a qualified clinician about your own care." %}
